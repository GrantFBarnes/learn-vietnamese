Vietnamese Alphabet

a, ă, â, b, c, d, đ, e, ê, g, h, i, k, l, m, n, o, ô, ơ, p, q, r, s, t, u, ư, v, x, y

Note the additional characters that are not in the English alphabet: ă,â,đ,ê,ô,ơ,ư

Note the missing characters that are in the English alphabet: f,j,w,z

The Five Accent Marks/Diacritics

The following marks are able to be applied to any vowels (including the ones above that already have marks).

Name Mark Example
acute ´ á
grave ` à
hook ̉ ả
tilde ~ ã
dot . ạ
